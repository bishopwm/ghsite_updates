Guarino Haskins Market Access accounces hire of
Kimberly Rawlins, SVP, Director of Market Access and Content

NEW YORK, FEBRUARY 3, 2015, 2016 — With the hire of Kimberly Rawlins, Guarino Haskins Market Access (GHMA) officially rolls out a key differentiating capability, Market Access and Content Expertise. GHMA, a member of The Healthcare Consultancy Group (HCG), is an integrated strategy consultancy and advertising agency specializing in market access.

“We are thrilled to announce that Kimberly is a member of our team and has a seat at the leadership table,” said President and Managing Partner John Guarino. “We’re poised to change the way market access marketing is done. From the first exploratory meetings to training account managers on tactics, from helping a brand launch to helping a brand pivot, our work is the most strategic it can be.”

The Market Access and Content capability lets the working relationship between clients and agency ramp up at warp speed. That’s because Kimberly and the experts on her team have built careers in market access — on the manufacturer, payer, and consulting sides of the business. They know the environment and the obstacles; they speak the language.

Denise Bottiglieri, PhD, CEO of HCG, said, “The importance of market access is growing and will only continue to grow. Kimberly is the absolute right person for this job. Her experience and expertise give Guarino Haskins a major edge in this core competency.”

Clients can tap the Market Access and Content team for a range of strategic support including: prelaunch strategy, payer value proposition development, message development and building an evidence base to support messages, product positioning, contracting strategy, data analytics, market research and more. The team’s experience spans the entire access and reimbursement spectrum, including but not limited to payer medical and pharmacy, specialty pharmacy, pharmacy benefit management, government payers and reimbursement support for practices and patients.

Kimberly joins GHMA with 17 years of experience in pharmaceuticals and biotech. She has worked in sales, sales leadership, brand marketing, and strategic payer marketing. Her most recent position was at McKesson Specialty Health, where she worked closely with manufacturers in business development. Prior to that she was director of managed care sales and operations at Amgen.

Healthcare Consultancy Group Announces Launch of
Guarino Haskins Market Access

NEW YORK, JULY 22, 2015 -- The Healthcare Consultancy Group (HCG), a division of the DAS Group of Companies, announces the launch of Guarino Haskins Market Access LLC, an integrated strategic consultancy and advertising agency specializing in market access.

Market access, the discipline of defining and communicating the unique value provided by medications and medical devices, is becoming increasingly important in today’s rapidly changing healthcare marketplace. Guarino Haskins will address this growing need by combining the experience of leading market access professionals with the power of award-winning creative talent, to provide consulting and policy analysis, marketing and health economics and outcomes research (HEOR) communications.

“Guarino Haskins will help us address some of the most critical issues our clients face today,” said Denise Bottiglieri, PhD, CEO of HCG. “Their ability to decipher and communicate payer issues will provide an important complement to our existing expertise in marketing, medical professional and regulatory communications.”

A member of HCG, with offices in Los Angeles and New York City, Guarino Haskins will pursue independent assignments in addition to bringing its payer competency to advertising, public relations and healthcare communications agencies throughout the DAS Group of Companies.

“Market access is the price of entry in pharmaceutical advertising these days,” said Dale Adams, Chairman and CEO of the DAS Group of Companies. “Brands can have the best professional and consumer campaigns, but unless they have market access, they won’t achieve uptake. In Guarino Haskins Market Access we have the right people with the right approach to help our clients achieve their goals.”

John Guarino, with over two decades of experience in market access on both the agency and manufacturer side, is Managing Partner, President of the company; and Roger Haskins, a veteran creative in market access communications, is Managing Partner, Executive Creative Director.

Guarino has deep experience in payer strategy and marketing. Most recently serving as Managing Director, Managed Markets at inVentiv Health, he oversaw the growth of a small division into an enterprise-level agency successfully winning and servicing payer business. Prior to that, as Director of Payer Segment Marketing at Amgen, he was responsible for implementation of all marketing to corporate payer customers including commercial payers, Medicaid, Medicare, specialty retail and mail order. In 2013, Guarino was named one of the 100 most inspiring leaders in the healthcare industry for his leadership in managed markets by PharmaVOICE.

“Brands live or die by their ability to secure and maintain access,” said Guarino. “At Guarino Haskins, we have a deep understanding of the complexities of the payer space and the ability to help clients define and deliver the value of their brand.”

Haskins has been in consumer and healthcare agencies for more than 20 years. As a creative director, he has a successful track record of translating complex payer issues into easy-to-understand and compelling communications. While at Entrée Health as Associate Partner, Creative Director, he helped achieve significant agency growth through impactful creative and managed that growth through sound leadership.

About Guarino Haskins Market Access
Guarino Haskins Market Access LLC is an integrated strategic consultancy and advertising company specializing in market access, which is the discipline of defining and communicating the unique value provided by medications and medical devices. With offices in Los Angeles and New York City, the agency is part of the Healthcare Consultancy Group (HCG) and a member of the DAS Group of Companies.

About Healthcare Consultancy Group (HCG)
The Healthcare Consultancy Group is a group of agencies that collaborate to enable healthcare companies’ medical affairs, regulatory, and marketing teams to better meet the needs of physicians, patients, and other stakeholders. HCG firms provide strategic and communications consulting, scientific branding and messaging, data visualization, and traditional and digital creative execution. HGC agencies develop and execute medical publication plans, stakeholder relationship and peer-to-peer education programs, regulatory communication plans, market access initiatives, and patient-centered development and communications programs. Headquartered in New York, and with offices throughout the United States and the United Kingdom, HCG agencies include Health Science Communications, Hyphen Digital, The Scienomics Group, The Chameleon Group, ProEd Communications and ProEd Regulatory, Accuverus, Scientific Strategy Partners, and Guarino Haskins Market Access LLC. HCG and its agencies are members of the DAS Group of Companies.

About the DAS Group of Companies
The DAS Group of Companies, a division of Omnicom Group Inc. (NYSE: OMC) (www.omnicomgroup.com), is a global group of marketing services companies. DAS includes over 200 companies in the following marketing disciplines: specialty, PR, healthcare, CRM, events, promotional marketing, branding and research. Operating through a combination of networks and regional organizations, DAS serves international, regional, national and local clients through more than 700 offices in 71 countries.

John Guarino

President, Managing Partner

John Guarino, with over 25 years of experience in market access on both the agency and manufacturer sides, is President, Managing Partner.

John brings a career of experience to the organization, including commercial payers, Medicaid, Medicare, specialty retail and mail order, global access teams, and trade, hospital systems, IDNs and access & reimbursement. His experience also includes new product launches, development and implementation of strategic marketing plans, and expertise in FDAMA 114 issues regarding formulary decision-maker strategy and marketing.

He has unparalleled experience in payer strategy and marketing. Most recently, as Managing Director, Managed Markets at inVentiv Health, he oversaw the growth of a small division within Palio into an enterprise-level agency with an unrivaled ability to win and service payer business. Eventually, he moved into corporate, heading up all of market access for the enterprise.

Prior to that as Director of Payer Segment Marketing at Amgen, he was responsible for implementation of all marketing to the corporate payer customers including commercial payers, Medicaid, Medicare, specialty retail, and mail order. He worked with the global team to create corporate strategy for all Amgen brands and was responsible for implementation of all marketing messages to the corporate customers.

Additionally, John led execution of marketing strategy and development of national POA, as well as national-, regional-, and account-level business plans and profiles.

John is an industry-recognized leader in the area of managed markets and is passionate about working in the space. He is an Adjunct Professor of Clinical Pharmacy at the University of Southern California, Graduate School of Pharmacy.

For his leadership in Market Access, John was named by PharmaVOICE as one of the 100 most inspiring leaders in the healthcare industry in 2013.

Roger Haskins

Managing Partner, Executive Creative Director

Roger Haskins has over 20 years of experience developing advertising campaigns and concepts. He began his career in consumer brand advertising at Euro RSCG. For 10 years he designed campaigns for everything from cars (Volvo) to doughnuts (Dunkin’ Donuts). Later, while at Merkeley & Partners he created his favorite work—the rebranding of TicTacs Chills, which you can find at a 7-11 near you.

Roger got his feet wet in pharma advertising on the professional side at Cline Davis & Mann starting in 2003. He worked on the global launch of Pfizer’s Caduet. Shortly after that he moved into Managed Markets at CDM and then Entrée Health, where he worked for 10 years exclusively in the market access space.

With training in fine art and a degree in biology, Roger is a left-brain, right-brain thinker. He is happiest when problem solving both a strategic and creative challenge.

Roger brings his award-winning creative experience to bear across multiple payer and media channels. He knows how to translate market strategies into effective payer communication strategies. His work has gained, improved, and maintained access for a variety of products. He is a firm believer that no matter who the audience is, knowing how to say something can be just as important as what you say.

Kimberly Rawlins

Senior Vice President, Director of Market Access and Content

Kimberly Rawlins has over 17 years’ experience in the pharmaceutical and biotech space. She brings to Guarino Haskins comprehensive knowledge of the manufacturer space, having worked in sales, sales leadership, brand marketing, and strategic payer marketing. Her experience includes the development and implementation of strategic marketing plans for the launch of new products in the payer space. She brings deep experience and understanding of the reimbursement and access issues facing manufacturers.

Kimberly joins Guarino Haskins from McKesson Specialty Health, where she worked closely with manufacturers in a business development role, focused on health informatics, health economics, and provider engagement solutions.

Prior to that, she was the director of managed care sales and operations at Amgen, where she led a team of analysts charged with the development, coordination, and measurement of strategic sales and reimbursement programs for the payer account management organization. In this role, she worked closely with cross-functional teams to create marketing messages and tools for the corporate accounts teams.

Kenneth Ng, Ph.D.

Vice President, Director of Medical Strategy

Dr. Ng has extensive experience in scientific strategy and medical education across a variety of therapeutic areas, including osteoporosis, heart disease, and cancer. He has supported a variety of brands at different stages of their life cycles from pre-launch with start-up companies to the promotion of mature brands. Dr. Ng has specific skills in thought-leader engagement, publication support, physician training, and conference/symposium programming.

Prior to joining the medical communications field, Dr. Ng earned his Ph.D. with distinction at Columbia University in bioengineering. His research involved close collaboration with surgeons and other medical professionals across a variety of technology platforms, including material testing, robotic surgery, and clinical imaging. In addition, Dr. Ng holds a Master of Science degree in Clinical Investigation from Cornell University that gives him insights into clinical research, regulatory trials design, epidemiology, and biostatistics.
